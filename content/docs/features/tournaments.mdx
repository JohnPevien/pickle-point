## MVP Entrants

Tournaments use fixed doubles teams.

Each team contains two registered player records. Team composition is set before bracket generation.

Guest and walk-in player records are valid tournament entrants. Player login accounts are not required to join an MVP tournament.

## Formats

- Single elimination
- Double elimination
- Round robin

## Bracket Library Decision

`brackets-manager.js` and `brackets-viewer.js` are reference-only for MVP. The app stores tournament teams, matches, scores, and bracket status in Convex and renders the current admin and public bracket views from that data.

## Lifecycle

- Draft
- Registration Open
- Registration Closed
- Bracket Generated
- Live
- Completed
- Cancelled

## Deferred

- Singles tournaments
- Random partner assignment
- Spin-the-wheel social pairings
