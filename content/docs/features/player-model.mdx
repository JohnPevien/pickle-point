---
title: Player Model
description: MVP player identity and skill rules.
---

## Rule

No anonymous participants. Every person in open play or tournament flow must have a registered player record.

## Required Fields

- First name
- Last name
- Owning Game Master workspace or venue
- Skill source: DUPR or manual
- DUPR rating when skill source is DUPR
- Manual skill level when skill source is manual

## Optional Fields

- Username
- Email
- Phone
- Gender
- Avatar or photo
- Emergency notes or private Game Master notes

## Future Account Linking

Player records should be claimable by a login account later, but account login does not block MVP participation.
