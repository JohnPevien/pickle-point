title: Open Play
description: Session lifecycle and operational flow.
---

## Session Statuses

- Draft
- Check-in
- Live
- Completed
- Cancelled

## Core Flow

1. Game Master creates an open play session.
2. Players are checked in from existing records or registered as walk-ins.
3. The app manages queue state and generates court assignments.
4. Game Master can adjust matches before starting them.
5. Scores and winners are recorded.
6. Live player view updates with courts, queue, standings, and recent results.

## Live Player View

- Current courts
- Up-next matches
- Queue position
- Sitting out status
- Standings
- Recent results
