## Session Statuses

- Draft
- Check-in
- Live
- Completed
- Cancelled

## Session Types

- Standard Open Play: flexible rotation-focused sessions for recurring club or venue play.
- Ladder Play: competitive open play where winners move up and losing players or teams move down courts, pools, or standings over successive rounds.

## Core Flow

1. Game Master creates an open play session.
2. Players are checked in from existing records or registered as accountless guest or walk-in records.
3. The app manages queue state and generates court assignments.
4. Game Master can adjust matches before starting them.
5. Scores and winners are recorded.
6. Live player view updates with courts, queue, standings, and recent results.

## Live Player View

- Current courts
- Up-next matches
- Queue position
- Sitting out status
- Standings
- Recent results
