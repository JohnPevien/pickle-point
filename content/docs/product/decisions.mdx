## Player Registration

All participants must be registered as player records before they can join open play or tournaments.

Walk-ins are registered by the Game Master during check-in. A player login account is not required for MVP participation.

## Skill Data

Every player must have either a DUPR rating or a manual skill level.

Manual skill levels:

- Beginner
- Novice
- Low Intermediate
- High Intermediate
- Advanced

## Tournaments

MVP tournaments use fixed doubles teams as entrants. Each team has two registered players, and the team composition is set before bracket generation.

Singles tournaments are deferred.

## Random Pairings

Random pairing and spin-the-wheel mode is a future social-event feature. It is separate from MVP tournament bracket generation.

## Matching Intelligence

MVP matching is rule-based first. AI matching is deferred until real session history exists.
