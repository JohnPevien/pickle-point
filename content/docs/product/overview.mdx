## Direction

Pickle Point is a realtime pickleball operations app for Game Masters: venue owners, club organizers, and people running open plays or small tournaments.

## MVP Scope

- Game Master account and workspace setup.
- Venue or club profile owned by a Game Master.
- Registered player directory with accountless guest and walk-in records.
- DUPR or manual skill data for every player.
- Open play session creation, check-in, queue management, and match generation.
- Live player view with queue, courts, up-next matches, standings, and recent results.
- Game Master control screen for running a session from one page.
- Session result entry and final leaderboard.
- Mini tournament setup for fixed doubles teams.
- Single elimination, double elimination, and round robin tournament formats.
- Browser-openable documentation at `/docs`.

## Not MVP

- AI matching.
- Singles tournaments.
- Random spin-the-wheel pairings.
- Cross-Game-Master player identity sync.
- Player account claiming.
