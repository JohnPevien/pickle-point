## Game Masters

Game Masters need authenticated accounts for workspace and venue management.

## Players

Player login accounts are not required for MVP participation.

Players are represented by registered player records first. Account claiming and cross-workspace identity sync are future features.

## Provider Direction

Recommended direction: Convex with WorkOS AuthKit for a stable B2B-friendly setup with social login support.
