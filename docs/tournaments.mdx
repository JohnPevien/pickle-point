## MVP Entrants

Tournaments use fixed doubles teams.

Each team contains two registered player records. Team composition is set before bracket generation.

Guest and walk-in player records are valid tournament entrants. Player login accounts are not required to join an MVP tournament.

## Formats

- Single elimination.
- Double elimination.
- Round robin.

## Lifecycle

- Draft.
- Registration Open.
- Registration Closed.
- Bracket Generated.
- Live.
- Completed.
- Cancelled.

## Seeding

Seeds are edited by the Game Master before bracket generation.

Rules:

- Seeds must be positive integers.
- Seeds are unique within each skill tier.
- Blank seeds are allowed.
- Generated brackets order teams by seed first, then registration order.

## Bracket Advancement And Corrections

Single-elimination brackets advance winners through dependent matches.

Double-elimination brackets generate winners, losers, and grand-final stages. If the losers-side finalist beats the winners-side finalist in the grand final, the app creates an if-necessary reset final.

Round robin generates all pairings within each active skill tier.

Score corrections are allowed when downstream dependent matches are still pending. Corrections recompute downstream pending slots. Corrections are blocked when completed downstream matches depend on the old result.

## Public Bracket

The public bracket view is available at `/[tenant]/tournaments/[tournamentId]` and groups matches by skill tier, bracket stage, and round.

## Bracket Library Decision

`brackets-manager.js` and `brackets-viewer.js` are reference-only for MVP. The app stores tournament teams, matches, scores, and bracket status in Convex and renders the current admin and public bracket views from that data.

## Deferred

- Singles tournaments.
- Random partner assignment.
- Spin-the-wheel social pairings.
