title: Player Model
description: MVP player identity and skill rules.
---

## Rule

No anonymous participants. Every person in an open play or tournament flow must have a registered player record.

Guest and walk-in players can participate without login accounts. The Game Master creates or selects an accountless player record for them during check-in or tournament registration.

## MVP Identity Model

Player identity is hybrid for MVP planning. The operating record is local to one Game Master workspace, so participation, skill data, match history, and tournament entries are scoped to that workspace.

Future login accounts may claim or link existing local player records. MVP does not automatically merge records across Game Master workspaces, even when two records appear to describe the same person.

## Required Fields

- First name.
- Last name.
- Owning Game Master workspace.
- Skill source: DUPR or manual.
- DUPR rating when skill source is DUPR.
- Manual skill level when skill source is manual.

## Optional Fields

- Username.
- Email.
- Phone.
- Gender.
- Avatar or photo.
- Emergency notes or private Game Master notes.
- Consent or opt-in state.

## Future Account Linking

Player records should be claimable by a login account later, but account login does not block MVP participation in open play or tournaments.

Future account fields:

- Username.
- Email.
- External auth identity.
