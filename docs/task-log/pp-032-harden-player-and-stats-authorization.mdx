## Status

Done

## Goal

Apply explicit owner/Game Master boundaries to player and statistics Convex functions, split safe public statistics projections from administrative player data, and fail player self-service closed until account-backed player linkage lands in Task 4.1.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Administrative player listing, creation, correction, and deletion require an active owner or Game Master.
- Player resource authority is derived from the loaded player row, never a browser-supplied tenant ID.
- Unauthenticated, suspended, player, and cross-tenant administrative access fails closed.
- Ownership is never authorized from browser-supplied user IDs, email addresses, or names.
- Identity-linking fields stay immutable through ordinary player/admin updates.
- Public statistics projections exclude email, phone, notes, opt-in state, WorkOS identity, token identifiers, and full player documents.
- Player self-service operations fail closed until the Task 4.1 `players.userId` link can prove ownership.
- All reads stay bounded and index-backed, with truncation flags where a defensive cap applies.
- The Convex access matrix records the hardened behavior.
