## Session Statuses

- Draft.
- Check-in.
- Live.
- Completed.
- Cancelled.

## Session Types

- Standard Open Play: flexible rotation-focused sessions for recurring club or venue play.
- Ladder Play: competitive open play where winners move up and losing players or teams move down courts, pools, or standings over successive rounds.

## Core Flow

1. Game Master creates an open play session.
2. Players are checked in from existing records or registered as accountless guest or walk-in records.
3. The app manages queue state and generates court assignments.
4. Game Master can adjust pending or in-progress matches.
5. Scores and winners are recorded.
6. Live player view updates with courts, queue, standings, and recent results.

## Fair Rotation

Open play match generation fills empty courts only and excludes players already in active matches.

Available queued and sitting-out players are prioritized by:

1. Consecutive sit-outs.
2. Total sit-out count.
3. Last played time.
4. Queue position.

Players selected for matches move to `playing`, increment `matchesPlayed`, clear consecutive sit-outs, and receive `lastPlayedAt`. Players left out of a generated round move to `sitting_out`, increment sit-out counters, and receive `lastSatOutAt`.

Paused players are not selected. A player returning from `sitting_out` is appended to the back of the queue.

## Manual Match Controls

Allowed controls include:

- Rename a court.
- Swap players between teams.
- Substitute a queued or sitting-out player into an active match.
- Cancel an unscored match.

The app blocks duplicate active assignments, outsider substitutions, and cancellation after scoring.

## Live Player View

- Current courts.
- Up-next queue.
- Sitting-out players.
- Paused players.
- Standings.
- Recent results.
- QR or shareable live link.
